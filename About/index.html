Our Story

Since 2023, we’ve trained over 5,200 students, supported 275+ women entrepreneurs, and raised awareness on cybersecurity in 10 communities. Beyond training, Oroki Hub fosters innovation, collaboration, and opportunities that strengthen Osun’s economy from the ground up.

About Us

Building Osun’s
Innovation

Oroki Innovation Hub is a community space designed to
bridge the digital divide, reduce unemployment, and
empower innovators. Through incubation, training, and
mentorship, we are shaping the next generation of changemakers.

Why Choose Oroki Hub?
