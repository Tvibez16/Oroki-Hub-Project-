About Us

Building Osun’s
Innovation
Ecosystem

Oroki Innovation Hub is a community space designed to
bridge the digital divide, reduce unemployment, and
empower innovators. Through incubation, training, and
mentorship, we are shaping the next generation of changemakers.

Our Vision

A thriving ecosystem where
every youth, woman, and SME
can access opportunities to
grow through technology and
innovation.

Our Mission

To bridge the digital divide,
empower entrepreneurs, and
drive inclusive innovation in
Osun State.

Why Choose Oroki Hub?

Our approach is unique because we combine the richness of local culture with the limitless potential of modern technology. We nurture creativity, support local entrepreneurs, and equip individuals with the skills to succeed in the digital economy.
